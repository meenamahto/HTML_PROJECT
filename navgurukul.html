Our Story

Started in 2016, Navgurukul runs one-year residential course for students from underserved and diverse
communities. Students after pursuing our course are awarded Certificate in Software Programming. The MOU
certificate is issued by DGT, Government of India .

In last five years, more than 250 students from marginalised areas have got jobs as Software Programs and have
donated back more than 28 Lacs in donation. We have diversified to add School of Design We have also started
initiatives to conduct experiments for popular products in a non-profit space.

Vision

We envision a world where everyone has access to an affordable world class education that enables them to live
up to their highest potential
